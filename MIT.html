MIT Sloan: Additional Information

Earlier this year, I participated in an online songwriting challenge called February Album Writing Month, where I wrote, recorded, and posted 14 songs in 29 days. My 8th song, Solo, describes my experience with single-sided deafness, a condition which has primarily impacted my relationship to music. I chose this composition for my additional MIT section to share a very personal story and highlight a passion I spend time on outside of work.
